Jiahao Li(李嘉豪)
I received from Guangdong University of Technology(GDUT) a bachelor degree and a master degree respectively in 2016 and 2019. Supervised by Prof. Ruichu Cai(蔡瑞初), I majored in Transfer Learning and Deep Learning while pursuiting my master degree. I have passed CET4 and CET6, and have a good command of reading English literature. I am developing a software called NetLabX to construct computational graph visually. If you want to contact me, please send email to jiahaoli.gdut@gmail.com or gahou.lee.edu@gmail.com.

NetLabX
NetLabX is a developing software designed to model something could be represented a nodal system. The software currently only has the most basic functions for building computational graph. More functions like interpretation and routing are still unavailable and being developed. Watch the following video and learn more about NetLabX.
EDA vSys
EDA vSys is a developed software for digital circuit simulation. It provides users with an environment to visually construct digital circuits. Moreover, it is convenient for users to observe the dynamic change of digital circuits in EDA vSys. Watch the following video and learn more about EDA vSys.
